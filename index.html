Takaful Principles

Islamic Principles of Mutual Protection

Takaful is based on the Islamic concept of Ta'awun (mutual assistance) where participants contribute to a pool to protect one another against loss or damage.

Shariah-Compliant

Ethical & Transparent

Our platform ensures all operations comply with Islamic finance principles, avoiding interest (Riba), uncertainty (Gharar), and gambling (Maysir).

Mutual Cooperation

Participants contribute to help those in need, embodying the spirit of brotherhood.

Transparent Contributions

All contributions and claims are recorded on the blockchain for complete transparency.

Shariah Governance

All claims undergo Shariah compliance verification before approval.
